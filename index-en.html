Why choose Baby Solid Food Helper?

Four core designs that make every bite safe and smart.

Science-based stages × absolute allergen exclusion

Strictly follows the four age stages — 4–6M, 6–8M, 8–10M, 10–12M — precisely managing daily meal counts and ingredient-variety limits. Once you mark an allergen, the system will never schedule any recipe containing it, in any plan — now or in the future.

From 4 months Allergen lock WHO-informed

One-tap weekly plan × smart shopping list

Each week automatically schedules a 7-day recipe rotation and instantly compiles a precise shopping list, with identical ingredients auto-totaled. Copy the list with one tap and send it to family or paste it into your notes — grocery runs have never felt so graceful.

Auto-schedule Shopping list One-tap copy

Editable weeks × next-week preview × preference learning

Switch freely between weeks to revisit every step of your baby's growth. Add, remove, or swap dishes and backfill reactions for any week — past, present, or the next-week preview you generate for shopping ahead. Report each dish (including "Not Eaten"), and the smart engine that blocks a recipe after 3 "dislikes" keeps learning your baby's tastes.

Editable history Preference learning Next-week preview

Feeding milestones × iCloud sync

All baby data, feeding plans, and preference records are encrypted and stored in your iCloud account via Apple's native SwiftData + CloudKit. Switch phones or reinstall the App — your data is never lost, and your privacy is safeguarded by Apple.

iCloud sync Encrypted Zero external servers
